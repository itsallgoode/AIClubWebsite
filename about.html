About the AI Club

Empowering students to explore, innovate, and lead in Artificial Intelligence at Kennesaw State University.

Our Mission

The AI Club fosters a collaborative community for students interested in Artificial Intelligence. Through workshops, hackathons, and research projects, we aim to make AI education accessible and inclusive.

Our Vision

To inspire the next generation of AI leaders and innovators by providing the tools and environment to turn ideas into reality.
